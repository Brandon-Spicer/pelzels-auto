Founded sixty years ago at Yakima Valley, Pelzel Auto Repair, LLC is a family-run shop providing full automotive repair services for cars, trucks, and RV’s. Our commitment to honesty and integrity motivates us to provide the highest quality work, and we are proud to have been voted Best Auto Repair Shop in 2011 by our community. We prioritize customer satisfaction, with our friendly staff here to provide a stress-free experience for you. Call us at 509-653-2251 and we can answer your questions and concerns about your vehicle.

About

Clint Isaacson, our shop owner, was born and raised in Naches, WA and has over thirty years of experience as an auto mechanic since graduating from Yakima Valley Community College. He is ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certified and is a member of the NFIB (National Federation of Independent Business).
